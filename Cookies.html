1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are:

  • Small text files stored on your device
  • Used to remember information about you
  • Sent to our server each time you visit
  • Help personalize your experience
  • Enable login functionality
  • Track usage for improvement

Examples:

  • Session cookies: Forget when you close browser
  • Persistent cookies: Stay for weeks/months/years
  • First-party cookies: Set by us
  • Third-party cookies: Set by partners (Google, Paystack)

4. How to Manage Cookies

4.1 Browser Settings

Chrome:

  1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  2. Choose: Allow / Block / Customize
  3. Delete existing cookies: Clear browsing data

Firefox:

  1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  2. Choose: Accept / Block / Block all
  3. Delete: History → Clear Recent History

Safari:

  1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  2. Choose: Allow / Block / Block all
  3. Delete: Develop → Empty Web Storage

Edge:

  1. Settings → Privacy, search, services → Clear browsing data
  2. Manage cookies and site data

4.2 Block Cookies

Consequences if you block cookies:
  • ✅ You can browse freely
  • ❌ Login may not work
  • ❌ Preferences not saved
  • ❌ Payments may fail
  • ❌ Some features unavailable

Recommendation: Allow essential cookies only

4.3 Delete Cookies

Steps:

  1. Open browser settings
  2. Find "Cookies" or "Browsing Data"
  3. Select time range: "All time"
  4. Check "Cookies"
  5. Delete
Result:
  • ✅ Cleared cookie history
  • ❌ May log you out
  • ❌ Reset preferences

5. Do Not Track (DNT)

What is DNT?

  • Browser setting indicating you don't want tracking
  • Sent as HTTP header to websites
Our response:
  • ✅ We honor DNT requests
  • ✅ Analytics cookies respected
  • ✅ Advertising cookies (none anyway)
  • ❌ Essential cookies still used (site won't work without)

To enable DNT:

  • Chrome: Settings → Advanced → Privacy → Send DNT
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy → "Always" for DNT
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → "Opt-out of tracking"
