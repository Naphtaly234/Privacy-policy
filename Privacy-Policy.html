6. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption in transit: HTTPS/TLS for all data sent between your browser and our servers.
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256 encryption in our database.
  • Authentication: JWT tokens for secure logins.
  • Passwords: bcrypt hashing – we never store your actual password.
  • Regular security checks: Vulnerability tests and penetration testing.
  • No plaintext secrets: We never store sensitive data in plain text.
  • Rate limiting: To prevent brute-force attacks on login.
  • CORS: We restrict which domains can access our API.
Remember: No system is 100% secure. If we ever have a breach, we'll notify you within 48 hours and take immediate action. We recommend using a strong, unique password.
